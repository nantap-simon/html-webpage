About BCSF

WWRC was founded in 2022. Welcome to Burning Coals Sisters Fellowship, a vibrant Christian community dedicated to empowering women in their spiritual journeys. Rooted in faith and sisterhood, our fellowship aims to inspire, uplift, and support one another as we grow in our relationship with Christ. At Burning Coals, we believe in the transformative power of fellowship. Our gatherings include prayer meetings, Bible studies, and social events designed to strengthen our bonds and deepen our understanding of God’s Word. Through shared experiences and encouragement, we ignite each other’s faith and foster a spirit of compassion and service. We invite women of all ages and backgrounds to join us as we explore our faith, engage in meaningful discussions, and serve our communities. Together, we are committed to making a positive impact, both within our fellowship and beyond. Join us at Burning Coals Sisters Fellowship—where hearts are ignited, faith is deepened, and lives are transformed!
