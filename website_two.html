Save a Child Foundation

Save a Child Foundation is a dedicated non-governmental organization committed to ensuring that every child has access to a balanced and nutritious diet. Founded with the belief that no child should go hungry, we strive to combat malnutrition and food insecurity among vulnerable children in our communities. Our mission is simple yet profound: to feed children three times a day with wholesome, nutritious meals that promote health and well-being. We work tirelessly to identify children in need, partnering with local schools, community centers, and families to provide reliable access to healthy food. Through our programs, we aim to not only nourish bodies but also inspire hope and empower young minds for a brighter future. At Save a Child Foundation, we understand that good nutrition is a vital foundation for education, growth, and overall development. With the support of compassionate volunteers, generous donors, and community partnerships, we are making strides towards a world where every child can thrive. Join us in our mission to save lives, one meal at a time. Together, we can make a difference!
