About WK

Wk was founded in 2022. Wetfir's Kitchen is a vibrant culinary oasis dedicated to bringing the rich flavors and traditions of Nigerian cuisine to our community. Inspired by the warmth of Nigerian hospitality, our restaurant offers an authentic dining experience that celebrates the diverse and delicious dishes of Nigeria. At Wetfir's Kitchen, we believe in the power of food to connect people. Our menu features a variety of beloved classics, from spicy Jollof rice and savory Egusi soup to mouthwatering Suya and fluffy Pounded Yam. Each dish is crafted with care using fresh, high-quality ingredients, reflecting the bold flavors and vibrant colors that Nigerian cuisine is known for.
